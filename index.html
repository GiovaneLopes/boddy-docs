Account Deletion Request

Last updated: 15/09/2025

If you wish to delete your account from Boddy, please follow the instructions below.

How to Request Account Deletion

To request the deletion of your account and all associated data, please send an email to:

gio.dev.personal@gmail.com

Information to Include in Your Email

Please include the following information in your deletion request:

  • Subject: Account Deletion Request - Boddy
  • Your full name: As registered in the application
  • Your email address: Associated with your account
  • Reason for deletion: (Optional)

What Happens After Your Request

Once we receive your request, we will:

  • Verify your identity to ensure the security of your data
  • Process your request within 30 business days
  • Delete your account and all associated personal data from our systems
  • Send you a confirmation email once the deletion is complete

Important Notes

  • Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone
  • Some data may be retained for legal or regulatory requirements
  • After deletion, you will no longer have access to the application

Contact

If you have any questions about the account deletion process, please contact us at gio.dev.personal@gmail.com
